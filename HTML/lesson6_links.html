"The Twelve Caesars" by Suetonius

Seutonius (or Gaius Suetonius Tranquillus) was born circa A.D. 70 and died sometime after A.D. 130. He composed a history of the twelve Caesars form Julius to Domitian (died A.D. 96). His work was a significant contribution to the best-selling novel and television series "I, Claudius." Suetonius' work includes biographies of the following Roman emperors:

  • Augustus
